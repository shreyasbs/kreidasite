Artificial Turf

Technical Specifications

  • FIFA & ITF certified Artificial turf
  • Superior quality yarn for better experience
  • Customizable specifications based on the client’s request


Advantages

  • Ease and readiness of use: Since the turf is manufactured and not grown you can get your place looking green in no time. There would be no requirement of pre preparation while the installation of this turf.
  • Low Cost and Maintenance: in contrast to grass growing on a field, these are easy to maintain. The rigorous practice of weeding, growing and watering are not to be concerned with after this installation. The cost also depends on the quality of turf one wants to use.
  • Durability: The green of the turf pops out the same way even after wear and tear. It’s only about giving it a good wash down and then you will have glistening green grass.
  • Sure footing: Players that play on the turf have found that they have a better foot hold while playing games as in stark contrast with grass which when wet becomes quite slippery.
